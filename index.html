Pop/R&B Artist from Nashville, TN

Kate has a voice that has the power to quickly silence and captivate a noisy room from the moment she sings her first note. Born in Pittsburgh, PA, Kate began performing and writing her own songs at age 11. Kate finally found her home in Nashville TN, where she began working as a session vocalist and co-writer. After fronting several bands, she finally decided to branch out as a solo artist so she could enjoy the freedom and authenticity of writing, performing, and producing her original music. Kate truly lives up to her nickname: “Ginger with soul”. She has been described as a force to be reckoned with, a vocalist with experience and skill well beyond her years, and one of the greatest undiscovered voices of this generation.
